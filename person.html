A very brief biography

I was born and grew up in anglophone Ontario, but both my parents were French, so I grew up bilingual

A longstanding love of books

Normally, parents celebrate if their child loves reading. Mine did too, until they started getting calls from teachers about me reading in class. Every day, I waged a silent struggle with the teachers. They would notice me reading and confiscate my book; I learned to come prepared and brought multiple books to school. Needless to say, the teachers were not pleased.

My favourite genres have always been a blend of mythology, historical and fantasy books, with science-fiction finding a place on my bookshelves in early adolescence.
